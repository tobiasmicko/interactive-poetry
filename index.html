Leticia: Interactive Poetry Experiments

An artistic investigation of poetic non-linearity and reimagination of the written word.

"There is no greater agony than bearing an untold story inside of you."

Maya Angelou

//Let accidents guide creative expression. By rearranging lines beneath, a masterfully crafted piece of writing can give birth to surprising results. Drag and drop the grey blocks to reimagine what has been written.

Leticia 1-4

Upon worst times of night to cower

On me befell the Witching Hour

My heart into my heels sunk deeper,

The Moon glaring at me—a faceless reaper.

Leticia 5-8

In ruminations dark and dreary,

I was just starting to get weary

I should have known that my mind’s dwellers

Are partial to midnight terrors.

Leticia 9-12

Right when falling prey to sleeping

Inside me, something started creeping

Did you hear that?It’s a promise—

Silence never was your solace.

Leticia 13-16

My paranoia—fierce and wrong

But skepticism doth burn strong

It was indeed what I most feared

Egging me on, inside my ear.

Leticia 17-20

Oh! How distinctly I remember!

Her soft voice sounding sweet and tender

Right when fear gave room to desire

She screamed: “Five! Fuck! Fire!”

Leticia 21-24

Bones! Bones! Ah! My old bones keep hearing

Her whispers, urgent but disappearing

Falling gently—ghostly feathers

To her voice I have been tethered.

Leticia 25-28

As upon me came the morrow

I found myself consumed with sorrow

When mind of mine called me a liar

For remembering “Five! Fuck! Fire!”

Leticia 29-32

Though mid-night’s always a tempest

Anticipation in me densened

My reason full of inhibitions,

But in my ears, the name,

Leticia.
